The Great Evangelical Recession

Video+
Design

Motion project for author John S. Dickerson's book, The Great Evangelical Recession. The book cover was already designed by John's publisher, so I used it as a starting point for the visual style. John recorded and provided the voiceover track, as well as selecting the music. I edited the VO, looped certain portions of the music to make it the proper length, and animated the project in Adobe After Effects.
