Résumé

View as PDF

I've been working as a designer full time since 2006. While my education was in graphic design for print, the majority of my experience has been in digital formats. I fell in love with the web while in school, and come from the era where you had to be able to both design and develop your work.

I believe that design isn't merely appearance; it's the combination of form and function working in unison with the goal of creating a positive emotional experience.
