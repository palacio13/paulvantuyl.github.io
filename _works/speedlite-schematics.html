I had the opportunity while working at iDC Photo Video to create illustrations for a series of Canon Speedlite training tutorials by Bruce Dorn. Each illustration is a schematic of the lighting layout for each photo created in the tutorial. This is a selection of some of my favorite pieces in the illustrations. You can see the finished videos and photographs from Bruce on Canon's Digital Learning Center.
