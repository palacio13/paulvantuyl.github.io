The Tallwave project for Aerial Sphere had some specific business requirements, and the end work that we produced for them included:

  • A single-page responsive website
  • An animated, explanatory video of their technology
  • A custom designed Keynote/Powerpoint presentation
  • Business blueprints and market plans for the next stage of their company

My main roles in the project were visual design, motion graphics, and editing. Over the course of the project I also gave design direction on other aspects, such as UX design and storyboarding for the video.
